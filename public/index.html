What is Imperator

Still the king until you get rich

Imperator is a full distributed, Ethereum Block-chain based, open source Game.

This UI is totally distributed as it is not hosted on a centrelized server, so no one can change its behaviour. Please verify the IPFS Hash (the application address)

Become a king

Become a king of an empty kingdom by buying it at ANY price.

You still the king until you get rich

Dethrone a king

Become the new king of an already taken kingdom, by snatching it. The kingdom will be yours, and wear your color, those by paying + 50% of it's price. 95% of the amount will be sent to to the actual king. but he will loose his kingdom :( .
